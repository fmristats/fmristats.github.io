Some notes on speed¶

On a Intel(R) Core(TM) i5-4440 CPU @ 3.10GHz with 4 cores the fit a signal model to data of an approximately 5 minute long FMRI session (230 scan cycles, 30 slices, 64x64 in-plane resolution) takes approximately: 4h 15min.

Fitting the population model is faster.
